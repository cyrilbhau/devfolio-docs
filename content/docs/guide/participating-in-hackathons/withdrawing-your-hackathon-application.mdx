There are two scenarios in which you can withdraw your application:

1. Before you get accepted to attend a hackathon.
2. After you get accepted to attend a hackathon.

<img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/eBDkDyLxD44cvbGjnKUs/blobs/lUyyN62lDWuRsIXURXqp/ScreenRecording2025-05-20at3.09.22PM-ezgif.com-video-to-gif-converter.gif" alt="How to withdraw hackathon application" />

## Withdrawing Before Acceptance

1. Log in to Devfolio and click on the profile toolbar in the top-right corner.
2. Click on <strong>My Hackathons</strong>.
3. Click on the hackathon you'd like to withdraw from.

<img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/eBDkDyLxD44cvbGjnKUs/blobs/5wDdRCYZdzy2Btz1phKD/image.png" alt="My Hackathons page" />

4. Click on the hyperlink <strong>Withdraw your individual application</strong>.

<img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/eBDkDyLxD44cvbGjnKUs/blobs/utql6iyNhE9HS7pvQ3f2/image.png" alt="Withdraw application button" />

5. Confirm your selection when prompted.

<img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/eBDkDyLxD44cvbGjnKUs/blobs/FbH1nw4QnQtdiIdklDy1/image.png" alt="Withdrawal confirmation screen" />

Once confirmed, your application will be withdrawn and you will be redirected to the application screen where you can submit a new one.



## Withdrawing After Acceptance

1. Log in to Devfolio and click on the profile toolbar in the top-right corner.
2. Click on <strong>My Hackathons</strong>.
3. Click on the hackathon you'd like to withdraw from.
4. Click on <strong>No, withdraw my participation</strong>.
5. Confirm your selection when prompted.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
Once confirmed, your application will be withdrawn. You <strong>will not be able to apply again</strong> using this Devfolio profile for the same hackathon.
</Callout>
