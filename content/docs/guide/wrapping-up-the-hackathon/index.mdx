## ✅ Final Checklist

Here are the key things to ensure as your hackathon concludes:

- [ ] Announce winners and update prize sections
- [ ] Send thank-you notes to participants, judges, and sponsors
- [ ] Collect and review feedback from the Feedback Tab
- [ ] Distribute reimbursements and certificates if applicable
- [ ] Share photo albums, blog posts, or recap videos
- [ ] Issue Onchain Credentials (optional)
- [ ] Archive the project submissions for future reference
- [ ] Reflect on what worked and document lessons learned for your next edition

<Callout title="Pro Tip" type="info">
  Keep your microsite live for a few weeks after the hackathon ends so participants can revisit projects and results.
</Callout>

## 📮 Post-Hackathon Communication

Use the **Admin Tab** to send out final updates, thank-yous, and feedback links.

---

## 📝 Retrospective

Organizing team members should meet to reflect on:

- What went well
- What could be improved
- How to make the next one better

Document this internally for continuous improvement.

---

🎉 That’s a wrap — congratulations on shipping your hackathon!
