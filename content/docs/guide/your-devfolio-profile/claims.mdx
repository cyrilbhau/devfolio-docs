### 🧢 How to Claim Schwag

1. Click on your profile in the top-right corner and choose <strong>My Claims</strong> or go directly to [devfolio.co/claims](https://devfolio.co/claims)

<img src="/assets/image-135.1.1.png" alt="Go to My Claims" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 1: Open your Claims</i></p>

2. Select the Schwag card.

<img src="/assets/image-133.1.png" alt="Select Schwag Card" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 2: Select Schwag card</i></p>

3. Click <code>Claim schwag</code> for the initiative you want.

<img src="/assets/pika-1682066192886-1x.png" alt="Claim Schwag CTA" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 3: Click to claim</i></p>

4. Fill out the necessary details and submit.

<img src="/assets/pika-1682066306949-1x.png" alt="Enter Details" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 4: Fill the claim form</i></p>

5. 🎉 Your schwag will be shipped soon!

<img src="/assets/image-18.png" alt="Success state for Schwag" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 5: Success</i></p>

<Tab title="Bounties">

### 💸 How to Claim Bounties

1. Click on your profile > <strong>My Claims</strong>

<img src="/assets/image-135.1.png" alt="My Claims" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 1: Open Bounties</i></p>

2. Select the Bounties card.

<img src="/assets/pika-1682066146228-1x.png" alt="Bounties card" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 2: Select bounty</i></p>

3. Click <code>Claim bounties</code>.

<img src="/assets/pika-1682066768151-1x.png" alt="Claim bounty button" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 3: Claim CTA</i></p>

4. Start the KYC process.

<img src="/assets/Screenshot-3236.png" alt="Start KYC" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 4: Begin KYC</i></p>

5. Verify your identity.

<img src="/assets/Screenshot-3237.png" alt="Verify identity" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 5: Identity verification</i></p>

6. Select document country and type.

<img src="/assets/Screenshot-3239.png" alt="Select document" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 6: Document selection</i></p>

7. Submit your documents.

<img src="/assets/Screenshot-3245.png" alt="Submit documents" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 7: Final submission</i></p>

8. Click <code>Confirm Wallet</code> to receive bounty.

<img src="/assets/pika-1682066851084-1x.png" alt="Confirm wallet" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 8: Confirm wallet</i></p>

9. ✅ You're all set — bounty will be sent!

<img src="/assets/pika-1682073331112-1x.png" alt="Bounty confirmation" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 9: Success</i></p>

<Tab title="Onchain Credentials">

### 🔗 How to Claim Onchain Credentials

1. Go to <strong>My Claims</strong> or click [here](https://devfolio.co/claims)

<img src="/assets/image-135.1.2.png" alt="My Claims entry" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 1: My Claims</i></p>

2. Select the <strong>Onchain Credentials</strong> card.

<img src="/assets/image-133.png" alt="Onchain credentials card" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 2: Select credential card</i></p>

3. Choose the credential you want to claim.

<img src="/assets/image-137.3.1.1.png" alt="Select credential" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 3: Choose the badge</i></p>

4. Link your Ethereum wallet.

<img src="/assets/image-139.png" alt="Connect wallet" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 4: Connect wallet</i></p>

5. Click <code>Continue with Ethereum</code> and complete the transaction.

<img src="/assets/image-146.1.png" alt="Ethereum sign transaction" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 5: Confirm transaction</i></p>

6. Confirm wallet (Devfolio will cover gas for eligible claims).

<img src="/assets/image-1.1.1.1.1.1.1.1.png" alt="Confirm wallet gasless" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 6: Gasless confirmation</i></p>

7. 🎉 Done! The credential will show up on OpenSea and EAS Scan!

<img src="/assets/image-2.1.1.1.1.png" alt="SBT success" />
<p align="center"><i>Step 7: View your credential</i></p>
