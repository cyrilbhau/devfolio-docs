You can find the <strong>Settings</strong> option in the dropdown after clicking on your profile in the top-right corner. This gives you access to various options to manage your account's visibility and security.

<img src="/assets/image-3.1.1.png" alt="Settings Tab Entry Point" />

---

## ⚙️ General Section

### 🖼️ Change/Add/Remove Your Avatar

You can delete your avatar by clicking on **Delete avatar**, or update it via **Upload new**. Once uploaded, you’ll be prompted with a resizing tool.

<Callout type="info">
  The image must be square, i.e., with a 1:1 aspect ratio.
</Callout>

---

### 🆔 Change Your Username

Update your username using the **Update username** button.

<Callout type="info">
  You can change your username only once every 90 days. It must be unique.
</Callout>

---

### 🔐 Change Your Password

Use the **Change password** button to reset your password.

<Callout type="info">
  Passwords must have a minimum of 8 characters. Use a mix of uppercase/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
</Callout>

---

### 🙈 Making Your Devfolio Profile Private

Toggle **Hide my Devfolio profile from public view** if you’d prefer your profile to be private.

<Callout type="info">
  This will hide your profile from search, QR access, and even hackathon organizers.
</Callout>

---

### 🔗 Link Your Ethereum Wallet

Click on **Link wallet** and follow the steps from [Linking Your Ethereum Wallet](./linking-your-ethereum-wallet.md).

---

### 🧪 Join The Devfolio Beta

Click on the **Join Beta** button to test unreleased features and send feedback to `beta@devfolio.co`.

<Callout type="info">
  Beta features may be unstable. Stick to Production if you prefer consistency.
</Callout>

---

Your QR code can be used to:

- Share your profile easily
- Check-in at Offline Hackathons

## 🗑️ Delete Your Devfolio Account

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to the Settings Tab
2. Enter your registered email
3. Confirm deletion

<Callout type="danger">
  Warning! This action is irreversible and will delete your entire Devfolio account.
</Callout>
