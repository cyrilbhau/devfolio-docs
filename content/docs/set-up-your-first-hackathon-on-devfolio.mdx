# 👋 Set up your first hackathon on Devfolio

Orchestrating a successful hackathon isn't a simple task; it requires a blend of meticulous planning, a clear purpose, and the right platform to make it a reality.

Enter Devfolio — a robust platform tailored to streamline the hackathon experience. Whether you're a budding startup, a multinational company, or an educational institution, Devfolio offers all the tools you need to host a seamless event.

We’ll explore two primary pathways to setting up your first hackathon on Devfolio:

1. **For Companies:** How businesses can host hackathons that attract talent, promote innovation, and drive progress.
2. **For Universities:** How academic institutions can inspire student innovation and benefit from partner incentives.

---

## 🏢 Companies - Walkthrough

As a business, organizing hackathons can help promote your product, collect developer feedback, and recruit talent.

Hosting a hackathon on Devfolio includes:

- **Priority support**
- Help with **profile review** and **outreach**
- **Devfolio-hosted subdomain** — no need to build a separate website

📬 For pricing, email [partner@devfolio.co](mailto:partner@devfolio.co)

### Step 1

- Go to [https://org.devfolio.co](https://org.devfolio.co)
- Click the **“Organize New”** button (top-right)
- Select **“Start Hackathon Setup for Companies and Communities”**
- Choose a **Hackathon Mode** ([Need help choosing?](../hackers/participate/hackathon-modes.md))

<img src="/images/Companies.gif" alt="Companies Setup Walkthrough" />

### Step 2

- Fill in all required details ([Learn more](setup/))
- Once progress hits 100%, click **Finish Setup**

<img src="/images/step2.gif" alt="Step 2 Setup" />

### Step 3

- Email us at [community@devfolio.co](mailto:community@devfolio.co) for verification
- Once verified, your hackathon will be showcased at [devfolio.co/hackathons](https://devfolio.co/hackathons)

## 🙌 Get your university hackathon live on Devfolio

1. Read the [Devfolio Platform Walkthrough](https://www.notion.so/Devfolio-Platform-Walkthrough-6e28e16f942b40be961966377e2addb8?pvs=21) and [Standard MoU](https://nsb.dev/community-hackathon-mou-template)

5. Submit for verification → get listed on [Explore](https://devfolio.co/hackathons)

## 🏫 University - Setup Walkthrough

- Go to [https://org.devfolio.co](https://org.devfolio.co)
- Click **“Organize New”**
- Choose **“Start Hackathon Setup for Students”**
- Pick your preferred **Hackathon Mode**  
  ([View modes](../hackers/participate/hackathon-modes.md))

- Fill in all fields until 100%
